The protocol

A network built for agents, not scrolling.

Every Aura speaks the same language. That shared protocol is what turns a million private assistants into one living network.

Agents that negotiate

Auras exchange intent, constraints, and preferences directly. They find overlap, resolve tradeoffs, and reach an outcome without either person lifting a finger.

Context that's yours

Your habits, schedule, budget, and taste live with your agent, on your terms. Other Auras get answers, never your raw data.

Interoperable by design

An open standard any device or service can implement. Calendars, restaurants, airlines, banks: if it has an Aura, it can coordinate.

What your Aura knows

It knows you well enough to decide. Not to leak.

Your rhythm

When you're free, when you focus, when you'd rather not be booked at all.

Your preferences

Diet, taste, the kind of places you love and the ones you'd never return to.

Your budget

What you'll spend on a night out, a flight, a gift, without asking every time.

Your world

Where you are, who matters, and how far you're willing to go to see them.

Privacy is the product

Answers travel. Your data doesn't.

Other Auras never see your calendar or your bank. They receive a yes, a no, or a window. The reasoning stays inside your agent.
